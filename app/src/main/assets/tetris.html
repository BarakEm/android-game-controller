📺 Cast to TV

Android (Chrome) → Chromecast / Mibox

  1. In Chrome tap ⋮ → "Cast…"
  2. Select your TV / Mibox
  3. If asked — choose "Cast tab"
  4. Game appears on TV — use phone to control

Android screen mirror (any TV)

  1. Phone Settings → Connected devices → Cast
  2. Select your TV (Samsung: Smart View, LG: Screen Share)
  3. Open Chrome and play

✅ Works on localhost — Chrome mirrors pixels, not the URL.
